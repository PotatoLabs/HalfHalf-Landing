Join the global community of advanced language learners looking to overcome their plateaus and better their speaking and listening skills. HalfHalf provides a platform for people to fairly exchange their language skills by timing each exchange. This is a common issue when exchanging languages with a partner because the person with high proficiency often naturally overtakes the exchange, leaving the other person unimproved. HalfHalf is best for people who already have a basic foundation of a language. Unlike other apps that focus on writing and reading skills, HalfHalf emphasizes human interaction, making it fun and productive to learn a foreign language. Learn by doing and have a good time!
